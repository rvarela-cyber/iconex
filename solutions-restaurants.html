Why Current Labeling Methods Are Failing Your Operation

Most labeling methods have too many materials and steps and are difficult for crews to handle.
When processes are time-consuming or manual, mistakes happen.

Current Method The Problem
❌ No label at all Prep instructions not close to prep area Distracting to look away from food
📋 Tearing tape to attach receipts Gloves get stuck to labels Dispenser easily misplaced
🏷️ Peeling labels off liners Gloves get stuck to labels Liner = waste to landfill
📎 Stapling receipts Staples can fall into food Stapler easily misplaced
✏️ Grease pencils/markers Easily misplaced Slow or difficult to read
